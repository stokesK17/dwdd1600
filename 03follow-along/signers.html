Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
The principal author of the Declaration of Independence.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence and later the 2nd U.S. President.
Benjamin Franklin
A statesman, inventor, and diplomat.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress; famous for his bold signature.
Samuel Adams
A leader in the American Revolution.
Richard Henry Lee
Introduced the resolution calling for independence.
Roger Sherman
Helped draft the Declaration and was part of the Constitutional Convention.

Each of these indivisuals played a significant role in the founding of the United States and is remembered for their courage in signing the Declaration, an act considered treasonous by the British Crown.
